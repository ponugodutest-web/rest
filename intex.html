your – YouTube API v3 Application

your is a web-based application that uses the YouTube Data API v3 to allow users to securely upload, publish, and manage videos on their own YouTube channels.

Purpose of the App

The purpose of your is to simplify YouTube video publishing through automation. The app uses Google OAuth 2.0 to request permission from users before performing any YouTube-related actions.

How your Works

  • User signs in with their Google account
  • User grants YouTube permissions to your
  • your uploads or manages videos on behalf of the user

Data Access

your only accesses YouTube data required for uploading and managing videos. No unrelated personal information is accessed.
