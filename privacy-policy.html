Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how this application handles user data while using the YouTube Data API v3.

Information Collected

The app collects OAuth tokens and basic YouTube account information required to perform actions requested by the user.

Use of Data

  • Uploading videos
  • Managing video metadata
  • Publishing YouTube content

Data Storage

OAuth tokens may be stored securely. No personal data is sold or shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

This app uses Google services and complies with Google Privacy Policy.

User Control

Users can revoke access at any time from their Google Account settings.
